Problem

Clinics receive symptoms, history, insurance details, and appointment context through disconnected forms. Nurses waste time reconstructing the case before they can decide who needs urgent attention.

Solution

CareFlow brings intake, urgency, completeness, and handoff notes into one calm queue so clinical teams can act faster without losing context.

  1. 01Patient submits symptoms and visit goal.
  2. 02CareFlow detects missing critical data and requests only relevant follow-up.
  3. 03Nurse reviews urgency, completeness, and waiting time in one queue.
  4. 04Disposition is assigned and the front desk receives non-clinical next steps.
