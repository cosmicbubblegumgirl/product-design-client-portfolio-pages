Problem

Returns are often treated as an afterthought. Shoppers feel unsure, support teams answer repetitive questions, and warehouse teams receive items with poor context.

Solution

ReturnReady captures the right return reason, resolution, evidence, and handling notes once, then reuses that context across customer care and warehouse teams.

  1. 01Shopper selects an order item.
  2. 02Portal explains return eligibility and policy reason.
  3. 03Customer chooses exchange, credit, refund, or repair.
  4. 04Warehouse receives reason, condition, photos, and handling notes.
