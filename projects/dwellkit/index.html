Problem

Rental maintenance requests often arrive with incomplete details, vague photos, and unclear urgency. Renters do not know what is happening and managers chase context.

Solution

DwellKit improves issue quality at the source, then moves requests through manager triage, vendor handoff, and renter status updates.

  1. 01Renter reports issue and adds guided photos.
  2. 02System checks emergency indicators and missing context.
  3. 03Manager assigns vendor with access notes.
  4. 04Renter confirms resolution or reopens with context.
