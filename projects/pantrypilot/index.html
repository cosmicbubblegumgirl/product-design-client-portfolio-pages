Problem

Repeat grocery shopping is predictable but mentally expensive. Shoppers forget staples, reject poor substitutions, and rebuild similar baskets every week.

Solution

PantryPilot remembers staples, explains recommendations, groups editable categories, and catches risky substitutions before checkout.
