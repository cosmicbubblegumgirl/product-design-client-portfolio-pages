Problem

Training teams want personalized learning, but most course tools make adaptation feel like a technical setup instead of a teaching decision.

Solution

LearnLoop starts with learning objectives, adds checkpoint logic, and explains learner recommendations in human language.

  1. 01Instructor defines course goal and skill objectives.
  2. 02Builder recommends lesson sequence and checkpoints.
  3. 03Learner completes checkpoint and reports confidence.
  4. 04Instructor reviews cohort friction and adjusts content.
